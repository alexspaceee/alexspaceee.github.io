In the shadows of the past, in light of the future



A troubled form along the cliff there hurries,
to the sure end he for ever scurries.
In his features the worries deep-set,
for the day his soul is to move ahead.
In his prime his thoughts there wandered,
wondering why, wondering when.
Yet the world spinned on another,
another day, forever and then.

This planet blue, the stage of any,
a home for all, the nest for many.
Now wings will spread and the gust will tell,
of the dawn in the making and the darkness as well.

The spark was born,
on the hearth it kindled.
Now it must spread,
among them commingle.
On Mars on Venus and on it must go,
to safeguard all life and cushion a fall.
