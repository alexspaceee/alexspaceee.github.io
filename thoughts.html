What are animals without without a body,
what is a thought without the memories,

that fashion, form and color it,

give it clothes and give a name to it,

a face to wear and a pulse to quicken,

and...

in the blink of an eye, and with a gust of wind,
the thought then vanished, bygone!
At last!
